ALPINE

Unturned's Biggest WW1 Server

Take control over the battlefield in a variety of vehicles, weapons and more on this awesome War RP server fighting grandiose combined arms battles to gain control over the map!


Alpine is successor to the famous Unturned server unTRUMPed. Although inheriting many of unTRUMPed's design decisions Alpine strives to improve the War RP formula in a number of ways for a better gameplay experience.

PLAYABLE DIVISIONS


  • Army

    Get close and personal with the enemy either as a Solider or Medic!
    Get access to deadly firearms and lead the other divisions to victory!

  • Armoured

    Ready the weapons and charge the enemy with a steel beast!
    Protect yourself from small arms fire and mow down incoming infantry!

  • Airforce

    Dominate the sky in a variety of exciting WW1 aircraft!
    Carry out bombing missions and scout ahead while defending your allied troops from incoming air attacks!

  • Navy

    Rule the sea with an armada of ships!
    Experience how it is to play large vessles with increadible firepower!
